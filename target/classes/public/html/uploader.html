Uploader

Uploader is a Java application running on the microscope computer to perform drift compensation and deconvolution and also upload your processed images to our server.
You can download the app from here: Uploader.jar

Please make sure to enter the right username while processing and uploading your experiments. It will remain consistent throughtout the pipeline.
